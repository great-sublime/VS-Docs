Modified Roadmap

This version adjusts the 19:5 fasting schedule so that it breaks on Friday at 11:30 a.m. (lunch), and then resumes on Sunday at 4:30 p.m.. Monday through Thursday still follow the usual 19:5 structure, with daily feeding from 11:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.. The weekend becomes more flexible, ending Sunday at 4:30 p.m., when the fast restarts for the new week.

    • Stop eating at ~4:30 p.m. each evening; fast overnight and into the next morning.
    • Break fast at 11:30 a.m., giving a consistent 19-hour fast / 5-hour feed each weekday.
  2. Friday Break (11:30 a.m.)
    • On Friday, you still break your fast at 11:30 a.m. as usual, but you do not resume fasting at 4:30 p.m..
    • The rest of Friday, Saturday, and Sunday morning/afternoon are more flexible (no strict 19:5).
  3. Resuming on Sunday (4:30 p.m.)
    • On Sunday, eat normally until ~4:30 p.m.
    • At ~4:30 p.m. Sunday, you restart the 19:5 fast to carry you into Monday morning, returning to the weekday schedule.
  4. Goal
